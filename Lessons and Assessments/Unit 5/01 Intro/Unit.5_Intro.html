Unit 5 Summary

You have made it to Unit 5! You are now at the final unit of this semester. Congratulations on your hard work to get here!

You have spent the first semester of geometry exploring a variety of geometric and algebraic topics. The purpose of the final unit is to ensure that you have retained the most critical concepts by completing two tasks.

  • First, you will use your geometry knowledge to complete a real-world application project.
  • Second, you will complete a self reflection to help you assess what skills and knowledge you gained this semester as well as what you should continue to review.
Objectives

By the end of this unit, you will be able to say:

  1. I can complete a real-life project that applies skills and concepts from Geometry A.
  2. I can reflect upon and explain both what I learned in this semester and what I should continue to review.
