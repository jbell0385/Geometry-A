Geometry A: Self-Reflection

Self-Reflection

For this assignment, you will need to reflect on what you learned in Geometry A, analyze the impact of your learning, and identify connections made. Your self-reflection can be completed either digitally and emailed to your teacher or submitted on paper. Make sure to use the prompts below and the rubric provided to help you with your writing.

  • Examine your overall progress through the semester and list two or more concepts that you understood well and two or more concepts with which you struggled. What will you do to improve upon the difficult concepts?
  • Analyze your learning experience using the online platform and give two aspects that helped you learn the information and two aspects that you feel were less helpful.
  • Describe how what you have learned in this course relates to other previous classes and or life experiences, your appreciation and understanding for geometry and your future goals in mathematics.

Ensure that you use mathematical terms you have learned in this semester appropriately and in multiple ways.

When you are finished, read through your reflection for your use of language before submitting it to your teacher including: use of transitions, correct grammar, spelling, and punctuation.
