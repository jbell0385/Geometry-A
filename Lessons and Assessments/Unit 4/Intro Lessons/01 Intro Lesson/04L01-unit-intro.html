Summary Power Standards Learning Targets

Unit 4 applies distance and ratios to geometric figures. After, you will apply the properties of lines and triangles that you have previously learned and incorporate new postulates to help you prove that triangles are similar.

The four main power standards that Unit 4 will focus on and will formally assess are:

  1. GPE.B.6: Find the point on a directed line segment between two given points that partitions the segment in a given ratio.
  2. SRT.A.2: Given two figures, use the definition of similarity in terms of similarity transformations to decide if they are similar; explain using similarity transformations the meaning of similarity for triangles as the equality of all corresponding pairs of angles and the proportionality of all corresponding pairs of sides.
  3. SRT.A.3: Use the properties of similarity transformations to establish the Angle-Angle (AA) criterion for two triangles to be similar. SRT.B.5: Use similarity criteria for triangles to solve problems and to prove relationships in geometric figures.
  4. SRT.B.5: Use congruence and similarity criteria for triangles to solve problems and to prove relationships in geometric figures.

Learning Target 1

Learning Target 2

Learning Target 3

Learning Target 4
