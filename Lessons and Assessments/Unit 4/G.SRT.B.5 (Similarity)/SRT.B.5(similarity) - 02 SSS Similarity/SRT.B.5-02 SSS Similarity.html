1. Side-Side-Side Similarity Postulate
Instruction Examples Video Self-Check

The Side-Side-Side (SSS) Similarity Postulate states that if all three pairs of corresponding side lengths have the same scale factor, then the triangles are similar.

Example 1.1

Decide if the three pairs of corresponding side lengths have equal scale factors in order to establish the SSS Similarity Postulate. If so, write a similarity statement.

  1. Find the scale factor of \(\dfrac {BC} {EF}\).
  2. Find the scale factor of \(\dfrac {AC} {DF}\).
  3. Find the scale factor of \(\dfrac {BA} {ED}\).
  4. If all the scale factors are equal, then the triangles are similar.

Example 1.2

Decide if the three pairs of corresponding side lengths have equal scale factors in order to establish the SSS Similarity Postulate. If so, write a similarity statement.

  1. Find the scale factor of \(\dfrac {PN} {WX}\).
  2. Find the scale factor of \(\dfrac {MN} {VX}\).
  3. Find the scale factor of \(\dfrac {PM} {VW}\).
  4. Since the scale factors are different, then the triangles are not similar according to SSS.
