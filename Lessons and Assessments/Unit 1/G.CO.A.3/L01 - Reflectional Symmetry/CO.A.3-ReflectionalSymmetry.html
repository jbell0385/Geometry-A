2. Reflectional Symmetry in Regular Polygons
Instruction Examples Video Self-Check

Regular Polygon

A polygon where all sides and angles are congruent to each other.

Lines of Symmetry in Regular Polygons

  • The number of lines of symmetry equals the number of sides in the polygons.
  • The lines of symmetry go through the center of the polygon. When there is an even number of sides, the lines of symmetry go from side to side and angle to angle. When there is an odd number of sides, the lines go from angle to side.

Lines of Symmetry in a Circle

When working with a circle, since the figure has no corners or sides, every line that passes through the center of the circle is a line of reflectional symmetry. Therefore, there are infinite lines of reflectional symmetry.
