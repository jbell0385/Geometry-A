Summary Power Standards Learning Targets

Unit 2 introduces foundational information about types of lines, angles and triangles.

  • You will begin by investigating lines to determine if two lines are parallel, perpendicular, or neither.
  • Next, you will examine angle relationships when two parallel lines are intersected by a third line.
  • Finally, you will apply these relationships as you begin to explore triangles.

The three main power standards that Unit 2 will focus on and formally assess are:

  1. GPE.B.5: Prove the slope criteria for parallel and perpendicular lines and use them to solve geometric problems.
  2. CO.C.9: Prove theorems about lines and angles.
  3. CO.C.10: Prove theorems about triangles.

Learning Target 1

I can prove the slope criteria for parallel and perpendicular lines and use them to solve geometric problems.

Learning Target 2

I can prove theorems and draw conclusions about parallel lines, transversals, and the angles they create.

Learning Target 3

I can prove theorems about triangles using given information, a diagram, or both.
